These widgets generally are used in BranchRootWidget but can be easily adapted for any other branch. 
/BarcodeRedactingWidget/
	This widget is concentrated on redacting barcodes. It must be initialized with barcode, then it can be used in any branch.
	Previously it was used twice - in ScaningWidget and in ScanedStorageWidget, but now it is only used in Scaning.
